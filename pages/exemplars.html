Zhuoyuan Zhang | 张卓元
中国经济学界的「常青树」，「经济研究杰出贡献者」国家荣誉称号获得者

Zhang Zhuoyuan (1933– ), hailed as China's economic "Evergreen", is the recipient of the national honorary title of "Outstanding Contributor to Economic Research", a CPC member, and Member of the Academic Divisions of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences.
Xiaokai Yang | 杨小凯
著名华人经济学家，中国社会转型问题的深刻分析家
华裔澳大利亚公民，莫纳什大学经济学系首席教授，澳大利亚社会科学院院士，1948—2004
Yang Xiaokai (1948–2004), a renowned ethnic Chinese economist, an incisive analyst of China's social transformation, a Fellow of the Academy of the Social Sciences in Australia, was the revered academic mentor of my master's supervisor, Prof. Sun Guangzhen.
Ludwig Heinrich Edler von Mises | 路德维希·冯·米塞斯

Ludwig Heinrich Edler von Mises (1881–1973), a leading representative of market liberalism, one of the most preeminent economic theorists of the 20th century, an American citizen of Jewish descent, and Visiting Professor at New York University.
Ronald H. Coase | 罗纳德·哈里·科斯
新制度经济学的创始人，1991年诺贝尔经济学奖得主
英国伦敦人，芝加哥大学经济学教授，1910—2013
Ronald H. Coase (1910–2013), founder of New Institutional Economics, recipient of the 1991 Sveriges Riksbank Prize in Economic Sciences in Memory of Alfred Nobel, a native of London, United Kingdom, and Professor of Economics at the University of Chicago.
