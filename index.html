Biography | 个人简介

Hi everyone, I'm Yu Xiaokun, a PhD student in Economics at the School of Applied Economics, Renmin University of China. I received my Master of Social Sciences in Economics from the University of Macau in 2025. My research has been published in peer-reviewed journals including China Economic Review and Economics & Human Biology. My academic field centers on Chinese economy, regional economic policy, and theories of regional development.

我是俞小坤，现为中国人民大学应用经济学院的在读博士研究生。我于公元二〇二五年毕业于澳门大学经济学系，获授社会科学硕士学位。我的研究成果已发表于China Economic Review、Economics & Human Biology等期刊，研究方向聚焦中国经济、区域经济政策与区域发展理论等领域。
